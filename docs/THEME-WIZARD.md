# Creating a theme from a site you love

The Theme Wizard turns a look you admire — a site, a screenshot, or just a
description in your own words — into an **original** theme for your Stillopress
site: its own colors, open-licensed fonts, and the right layout. You then refine
it by chatting, and keep it when it feels right.

Find it under **Theme Wizard** in the site sidebar.

## Three ways to start

- **A site you like** — paste a URL. The wizard reads the site's *feel* and drafts
  a theme in that spirit. *(Requires server-side screenshot capture; if that's
  not enabled on your server, use a screenshot upload instead — see below.)*
- **A screenshot** — upload a PNG, JPEG, or WebP of any site or design you like.
  This always works.
- **Just describe it** — no reference at all. Type a mood, an industry, and a
  couple of adjectives (e.g. *"a warm, calm wellness studio — earthy tones, a
  gentle serif, lots of air"*) and the wizard designs from words alone.

A short optional **direction note** ("warmer", "more editorial") steers the first
draft.

## Refining it

Once you have a draft you'll see:

- a **live preview** on the right — exactly what will publish, with a
  desktop/mobile toggle;
- a **chat** on the left with the theme's name and a short "design read"
  explaining the choices.

Refine it in plain language — type feedback or tap a quick chip:
**warmer · more contrast · quieter headings · rounder · airier**. Each nudge
re-designs the theme and reloads the preview. Keep going until it's right.

## Keeping it

Hit **Keep this theme**. It's saved as a normal theme on your site and opens in
**Theme Studio**, where you can fine-tune any token by hand, then activate it
from the **Themes** page like any other theme.

## Inspired, not copied — how it works

The wizard never copies a reference. This is guaranteed, not just asked for:

- **Colors are shifted.** The palette is nudged in hue and lightness so it reads
  as a distinct sibling — never a reference's exact brand color.
- **Fonts are substituted, never taken.** The wizard reads the *character* of the
  type ("high-contrast serif", "condensed grotesque") and picks an
  **open-licensed** font of that character from a curated allowlist. A reference
  site's licensed font can never end up in your theme — the wizard has no way to
  emit a font name at all.
- **No imagery, logos, or copy.** The wizard only ever produces **design tokens**
  (colors, type, spacing, radius, shadow, layout). It has no place to put a
  reference's images, logo, or wording, so none of that is ever reproduced.
- **No brand impersonation.** It's instructed never to clone a recognizable,
  trademarked brand so closely that it could be mistaken for it.

The result is a theme that clearly *feels* related to what inspired you, but is
your own — and is safe to publish.

## Cost

Each design or refinement is a single AI call charged against your tenant's
monthly token budget (typically a few hundred to a couple thousand tokens — a
fraction of a cent to a few cents). The running total is tracked per session.
